Banana Pancakes

Description

Wake up on the right side of the bed with a stack of sweet, cozy, and simple banana pancakes. This top-rated banana pancake recipe is easy to make and it comes together in just 15 minutes, so you don't have to wake up early to enjoy a satisfying breakfast. Banana Pancakes Ingredients

If you have ready-to-use bananas and a few kitchen staples, you already have everything you need to make perfect banana pancakes. Here are the ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • An Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas
